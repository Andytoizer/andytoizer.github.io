Barst Mukamal and Kleiner has been a leading immigration law firm for the past decade. We believe that America was built on foundations of liberty by those who were politically and religiously prosecuted. The Bast Mukamal and Kleiner foundation makes it their mission to give every immigrant an opportunity to come through our borders.

The majority of immigrants whom aren't being allowed to enter our country are of a low socio-economic class. There is no reason that someone who has more money should have a better chance to live in our country than a poor person. We believe that money is not a characteristic of a good contributor to society and so we at Barst Mukamal and Kleiner strive to make immigration and equal opportunity to anyone in the world.

Once through the immigration process many immigrants are then stuck without many resources because of their lack of knowledge as to how The United States run. Our foundation raises money to not only help pay for legal work for those who cannot pay it, but also to fund programs that help immigrants grow accustom to the ways of our country.
