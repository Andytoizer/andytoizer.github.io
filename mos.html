Silence

The average person checks their phone 100 times a day

Our mission is to raise awareness for youth individuality. Our youth is bombarded with "fake news", ads, and social media to such an extent that we aren't taking the time to look down from our phone. Moment of Silence challenges youth from around the world to film two minutes of their life in order to raise awareness in our youth to start thinking for themselves rather than believing everything they read online.
